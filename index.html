About Us

Started in 2021, Batang Bagumbong Sports Fest (BBSF) is an annual inter-subdivision sport program of Sangguniang Kabataan Barangay 171, Caloocan for its youth.

We want to promote and achieve peace by means of sports. Instill the spirit of discipline, teamwork, excellence, fair play, solidarity, sportsmanship, and other values inherent in sports.
